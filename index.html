What is articulation disorder?

A typical production of speech sounds characterized by substitutions, omissions, additions or distortions that may interfere with intelligibility.

How articulation disorder is treated?

Mild speech problems can go away with time. For severe speech problems that do not come from a physical problem, a child will need to begin speech therapy treatment.

How articulation disorder is diagnosed?

To diagnose an articulation disorder, a child will need to see a specialist such as a speech-language pathologist.

If age is a barrier for sppeech therapy?

It's always better to start treatment early. But it's never too late to begin. After all age is just a number.
